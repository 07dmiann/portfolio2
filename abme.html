Bio

I've been passionate about drawing for as long as I can remember. While I started with traditional art at a young age, I discovered digital art a few years ago and have been exploring it ever since. Over time, I’ve shared my work on social media, where you can see my progress and growth as an artist.

Most of my art is fanart inspired by my favorite games, books, and anime. Sometimes, my pieces reflect my thoughts and emotions, making art not just a hobby but also a form of self-expression. I'm always looking for ways to improve—experimenting with new styles, refining my techniques, and pushing myself to become better with every piece I create.
